It's Beginning to Look a Lot Like Christmas

Though we started our Esty shop in 2020, we didn't create Christmas specific items for sale last year. Despite this, we did design and gift a few different Christmas items. Well 2021 is a year for growth, including our first in person selling opportunity.

In just about a week and a half, we'll be attending a local elementary school's annual Winter Wonderland & Santa event. We are taking the plunge and hosting a booth, and we definitely needed to include some Christmas and winter holiday items.

We designed a Christmas Tree light that has multiple variations and options to choose from. In addition to these trees, we've also created some snowflake ornaments and mini ornaments. We'll round out the product offering with a few of our wall art options including custom pennants and state outlines themed around Missouri and our local sports teams.

We're busy creating inventory for the event and are looking forward to what we hope will be our first of many succesful events!
